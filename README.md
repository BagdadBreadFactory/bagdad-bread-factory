## 🥐 About the Project

**Bagdad Bread Factory & Sweets** is a modern landing page designed to give local customers an effortless browsing and ordering experience. Built with pure vanillla web technologies, it offers high performance, fast load times, and smooth mobile interactions without the weight of heavy frameworks.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* **📱 Fully Responsive Design:** Optimized for seamless performance across smartphones, tablets, and desktop displays.
* **📲 Quick Action Buttons:** One-tap **Call Now** and **WhatsApp Chat** buttons for instant customer orders and inquiries.
* **🍔 Mobile-Friendly Navigation:** Touch-optimized mobile menu and interactive header layout.
* **🖼️ Interactive Product Gallery:** High-resolution image showcase highlighting daily fresh bakes, bread, and traditional sweets.
* **⭐ Customer Reviews & Testimonials:** Social proof section displaying ratings and feedback from satisfied customers.
* **📊 Business Metrics & Statistics:** Animated counters displaying years of service, items baked daily, and happy customers.
* **🔍 Built-In SEO Optimization:** Structured meta tags, Semantic HTML5 markup, and Open Graph protocol ready for social media indexing.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** [HTML5](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/HTML) (Semantic elements)
* **Styling:** [CSS3](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS) (Flexbox, Grid, Custom Variables, Animations)
* **Scripting:** [JavaScript (ES6+)](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript) (DOM manipulation, mobile menu toggle, scroll triggers)
* **Fonts & Icons:** Google Fonts & Font Awesome / Line Awesome (if applicable)
